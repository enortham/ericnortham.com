About Me

I’ve lived in Mexico City since 2012, where I’m always trying new restaurants and staying active—gymnastic rings are my current go‑to, but I also run, lift weights, and play sports when I can, especially anything with a racket or paddle. Originally from Washington, DC, I’ve been abroad since 2005—six years in Buenos Aires, two years traveling the world, and now deeply rooted here. I also speak Spanish. At home it’s me, my 12-year-old son, and two cats: Isidro and Poof.

I studied Computer Science at UMBC and Takushoku University in Tokyo, where I eventually learned to speak Japanese well (and then subsequently forgot it). I still love to program, mainly in Ruby, but I'm equally passionate about design, product, entrepreneurship, writing and everything AI.

My Work

I'm the Founder and CEO of EasyBroker, which I started in 2007 while living in Buenos Aires. We've built Mexico's leading Real Estate CRM, 100% bootstrapped.

I came to Mexico because EasyBroker took off here, and I wanted to be closer to my clients and truly understand the market. Recently, we launched Pincali, a real estate portal with an ambitious goal: to become the leading source for finding homes to buy or rent in Mexico.
